Technical Faq

What is the cost of renting a battery?
Renting duration will be effective exactly 3 minutes after a power bank has been rented. First time users will enjoy a 30 minutes free rental time. A rental period that is less than 60 minutes will be rounded up as an hour. The time and day the power bank is returned to the station will mark the conclusion of the rental period. The rental fee is $0.99 per hour up to a maximum of $4.99 per day. You can rent the battery for a maximum period of 2 days. After 2 days your maximum continuous rental period will expire, and your portable power bank will be considered purchased.

What happens if I do not return the battery?
After the period of 2 days, failure to return the rented battery will cause battery to be regarded as purchased. If this happens, a sale's fee of twenty-five dollars USD ($25.00) plus tax will be charged to your credit card. The battery can be charged with a standard Micro USB cable plugged into a wall outlet orcomputer.

BATT2GOwill not be liable for any damages or losses of any equipment left unattended while using our courtesy charging cable.

Where can I return the battery after I am done using it?
You can return your battery at any BATT2GO Station that has an empty slot. BATT2GO stations locations are available on the app.

What happens if I lose a battery?
If you lose a battery, please report the issue to us immediately to avoid any additional fees. Wewill charge youtwenty-five dollars USD ($25.00) plus tax for every expired/lost power bank.
